Offer libations to the dead

One of the most popular activities in the Underworld is offering libations to the dead in order to meet the spirits of those who you wish to see. There is a simple process of digging "a trench of about a forearmâ€™s depth and length and around it poured libations out to all the dead, first with milk and honey, and then with mellow wine, then water third and last, and sprinkled glistening barley over it all" (175). Afterwards, a couple of vows and prayers will invoke the spirits of the dead to come meet you. It is a wonderful experience for many and the possibilities of who to invoke are endless!
