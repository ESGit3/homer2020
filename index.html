About us

The Kingdom of the Dead is the largest and only area where the dead reside. We take pride in giving fair treatment to each and every one of our deceased. During your visit to the Kingdom of the Dead, you will be able to consult some of the greatest seers to have existed, get to know your ancestors, get advice for your journeys abroad, and even watch the suffering of others. You will be able to take in a full experience of the 3 levels of the Underworld during your stay. Look for the other pages to learn more about what you may experience during your trip.
